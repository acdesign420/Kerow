About us

What do we do

Kerow tattoo studio has been established in 2016 by Viktor and his partner in crime Lucia. Since day one, it’s main goal has been to give our customers experience, they will never forget and never regret. We understand that getting a tattoo is a serious commitment usually lasting entire life, which is why we want to give our customers something, they will find beautiful even after decades and give them reason to always come back to us by giving them something extra. We look after our customers with professional guidance and intense care. Over the years we grew in numbers, we do more styles, but the core concept remains the same - to give you something you will love your entire life.
