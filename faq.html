Yes, getting inked is painful, but it's manageable. Everything depends where you would like to have tattoo.
More painful tattoos are on places where the skin is softer and more sensitive (neck, inner side of legs, arms and groin)
and on the places, which are close to the bone (collar bone, rib cage, elbows and knees).
The pain can be described as soft burning and cutting.
Everybody has different threshold of pain so the pain is different on each person.

It's very individual. Healing of tattoo last from 10 to 14 days.
After this period tattoo should be protected from sunshine. We recommend hydratate the place, which was tattooed.

After-care is very important, first days it's everything about increased hygiene.
Those days you should use antibacterial soap, avoid contact with dirty things,
do not swim in lakes and swimming pools and do not touch tattoo with dirty hands.
Do not scratch the place. Do not scratch scabs. In case of tattoo, after 3 days you should cream
tattoo with thin layer of healing cream or after-care product, minimally 3 times a day.
